In this directory we place the alerts we set in order to keep watching a stock we sold
The chat will observe the list of trades.
For each trade, taking into account
- sell price
- sell time
- buy price
- buy time
- relevant support and resistance levels,
- current price relation with short and long term MA
- volume trend between sell date and current date
- short term volume trend
- long term volume trend
- short term MA trend
- long term MA trend

it'll give it opinion whether the stock looks like it's a good idea to get back in, and for how long
