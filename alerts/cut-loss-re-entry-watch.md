# Cut-Loss Re-Entry Watch

This file tracks stocks we sold at a loss, monitoring them for potential re-entry opportunities.

## Analysis Framework

For each sold stock, consider:
- **Sell price and sell time** - Reference point for the loss
- **Buy price and buy time** - Entry context
- **Support and resistance levels** - Identify constructive pullback zones
- **Moving average position** - Is price above/below 20/50/200 SMAs?
- **Volume trends** - Between sell date and current date; short-term vs long-term
- **Momentum indicators** - 21d, 63d, 252d returns; drawdowns
- **Technical structure** - Higher highs/lows, bases, flag patterns

## Re-Entry Criteria

- Price has formed a constructive pattern (support hold, higher low)
- Volume trend supports the recovery
- Moving averages are aligned above the stock (in uptrend)
- Drawdown from highs is meaningful (opportunity zone)
- Time decay reduces the impact of the original loss
