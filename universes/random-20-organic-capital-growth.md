# Random 20 Organic Capital Growth

Randomly selected 20-stock universe for an organic capital growth setup.

## Sampling Method

The universe was sampled from tickers present in the local
`data/stock/prices/daily/2021/` dataset.

Filters:

- uppercase alphabetic symbols only
- 1 to 5 characters
- excludes known ETF, leveraged fund, ETN, and crypto-fund tickers already used
  as portfolio wrappers

Random seed:

```text
20260707
```
